## Workflow

### First Time Setup
1. Navigate to a SAPO Emprego job listing
2. **Double-click** the form OR click the extension icon to open the popup
3. Fill in your information (name, email, phone, photo, CV)
4. Click **"Save Data"** to store your information
5. Click **"Fill Form"** to fill the current job application

### Quick Applications (After Setup)

1. Navigate to a SAPO Emprego job listing
2. **Single-click** anywhere on the form
3. Form fills automatically! âœ¨
4. Review the filled information and submit

### Updating Your Information
1. **Double-click** the form OR click the extension icon to open the popup
2. Scroll down to the edit form
3. Update any fields you want to change
4. Upload new files if needed (optional if already saved)
5. Click **"Save Data"** to store the new information
6. Use **single-click** on the form to apply the updated data

## Troubleshooting

**Form not filling?**
- Make sure you're on a SAPO Emprego job application page
- Check the browser console for any errors
- Try double-clicking to verify your data is saved

**Need to reset everything?**
- Open the browser's extension settings
- Clear the extension's storage
- Click the icon again to set up from scratch
